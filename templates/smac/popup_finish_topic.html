What do I do next?

Before your topic is activated you will have complete payment by clicking the "Complete Payment" button below. After we confirm your payment, your topic will immediately start collecting data.

When can I see my first report?

Reports will be generated as your data comes in. Although reports vary depending on the rules you create, in most cases statistics and analysis are visible within a hour after paying for topic
